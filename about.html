About GreenPass

Reimagined Tourism: A Smart Integrated Ecosystem for Post-Pandemic Recovery

Project Overview

The tourism industry, a major contributor to global economic development and cultural exchange, faced severe disruption due to the COVID-19 pandemic. This exposed significant limitations in traditional tourism systems such as fragmentation, lack of personalization, and poor sustainability practices.

In response, GreenPass proposes a Smart Integrated Tourism Ecosystem that leverages advanced technologies including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), cloud computing, and data analytics to create a unified, intelligent platform.

The system connects tourists, local communities, service providers, and administrators — enhancing user experience through AI-driven personalized recommendations, real-time communication, and seamless booking services.
