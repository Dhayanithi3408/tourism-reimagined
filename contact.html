Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI recommendation system work?
Our AI analyzes your travel preferences, history, and real-time contextual data to suggest personalized destinations, accommodations, and activities with 95% accuracy.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes. All transactions are encrypted using enterprise-grade security protocols. We use JWT authentication and secure payment gateways to protect your data.
What is the IoT environmental monitoring?
IoT sensors at destinations track crowd density, air quality, temperature, and resource usage in real-time to promote sustainable tourism and prevent overcrowding.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?
Yes, bookings can be modified or cancelled through your dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by destination and accommodation type.
